The Arrangement

Kimball joins as a business development and client management partner. He sources and manages his own book of clients, earning a revenue share on every dollar that book generates. Two structures are proposed below — one optimized for maximum cash, one for long-term ownership.

Offer A

Pure Rev Share

No equity. Maximum cash. 90% rate at $40k portfolio.

Offer B

Rev Share + Equity

Up to 10% ownership. Starts at 30%, caps at 80% at $100k portfolio.
